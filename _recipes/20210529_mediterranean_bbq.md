Chickpea Burgers

From: [Eva Agha](https://www.thecuriouschickpea.com/vegan-meatballs-in-roasted-red-pepper-sauce/)

MAKES 10-12 / 10min prep / 10min grill

- 2 cups cooked brown rice
- 1 cup pecans, peanuts, or other nut
- 4 cloves garlic, peeled
- 30oz chickpeas, about 2 cans
- 1/2 cup nutritional yeast or zaatar
- 2 tbsp tomato paste or similar (e.g. nduja)
- Generous salt and pepper for seasoning

Combine rice, nuts, and garlic in a processor until finely ground. Add most of the chickpeas and other items, and process into a mixture. Add the rest of the chickpeas and pulse until coarsely chopped in.

Form balls or patties and sear on a grill at ~425 degrees for several minutes on each side.
